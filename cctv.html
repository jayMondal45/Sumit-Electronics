With years of expertise in surveillance technology, we provide end-to-end services, including installation, maintenance, repairs, and upgrades of CCTV systems. Our team ensures your system is optimized for clear monitoring, ease of use, and maximum reliability.

Our CCTV Services Include:

1. CCTV Installation
  • Home Security: Secure your property with high-definition cameras for indoor and outdoor monitoring.
  • Business Surveillance: Monitor offices, warehouses, and retail spaces with strategically placed cameras.
  • Customized Setup: Tailored placement and configuration to ensure optimal coverage.
  • Smart Features: Integration with mobile apps, motion detection, and night vision capabilities.
2. CCTV Repairs and Maintenance
  • Troubleshooting and fixing malfunctioning cameras.
  • Replacing or upgrading damaged components like cables, DVR/NVR systems, or lenses.
  • Routine maintenance to ensure your system operates at peak performance.
  • Cleaning and adjusting camera angles for optimal video clarity.
3. System Upgrades
  • Transitioning from analog to advanced IP-based systems.
  • Upgrading to 4K or HD cameras for better video quality.
  • Adding advanced features like AI-powered motion detection, facial recognition, and smart notifications.
4. Remote Monitoring Setup
  • Configuration of remote access to CCTV feeds via smartphones, tablets, or computers.
  • Training on how to use mobile apps and monitoring software for real-time updates.
5. Comprehensive Consultation
  • On-site assessments to determine the best camera types and placement.
  • Recommendations based on property size, lighting conditions, and security goals.
